About Ghibli World

Discover the Universe of Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli has enchanted audiences worldwide since 1985, crafting animated worlds of breathtaking beauty and profound emotion. From the windswept landscapes of Nausicaä to the spirit-filled bathhouses of Spirited Away, every Ghibli film is a masterpiece of imagination.

This site is your guide to the films, the unforgettable characters, the sweeping soundtracks by Joe Hisaishi, the prestigious awards, and heartfelt community reviews that celebrate this extraordinary studio's work.
